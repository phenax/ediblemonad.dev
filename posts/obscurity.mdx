## What is information worth?

If you had to put a price tag on it, what would it be? I guess it depends.
I believe we can evaluate the "price" of information using the following factors among other things.
- utility: What you can use it for. Nuclear launch codes seem very valuable
- rareity: how rare the thing is.
- longevity: does it stay forever or is it temporary. (persistance of internet)
- consumability: does consuming it affect its value? You can learn the information and there'll still be the same amount of information left for others to consume.
