# Mr. Bungle is real art

A band like Mr. Bungle is rare because given how hard it is to make enough money to survive as a band, I'm surprised how more bands don't lean towards mass appeal. But Mr. Bungle is comfortable with just finding an identity in making the songs that they want to make, even if that identity involves including ${partials.linkExternal "https://youtu.be/nE6UUg0Nenc?si=-g8dWEof9tVkZWoG&t=448" "the track 'Noises'"} in your album.

For people who want to get into them, the album order I enjoy is California -> Mr. Bungle -> Disco Volante -> everything after that. To me that is ordered by weirdness because I like starting comfortable.
