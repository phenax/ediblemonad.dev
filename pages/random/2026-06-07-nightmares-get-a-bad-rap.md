# Nightmares get a bad rap

Nightmares are so much fun! It's the most immersive horror movie you've ever watched. Or a comedy-horror movie about how you're in school again standing naked in front of all your classmates because you forgot to wear your clothes and you realize that this is more than just about your clothes. You exist in a perpetual state of shame with no end in sight. You talk about acceptance but then use every chance you get to criticize yourself for... Okay, I take it back, nightmares can be bad I guess.
